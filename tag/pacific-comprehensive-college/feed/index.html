Celebrating Excellence: Adenike Adepetu Emerges as First Runner-Up in I Beg to Differ Debate Competition.

CELEBRATING EXCELLENCE: Adenike Adepetu Emerges as First Runner-Up in Season 7 of I Beg to Differ Debate Competition.

By

Mike – Femi

We are thrilled to share some exciting news with our esteemed community! UPSS Comprehensive College is proud to announce that one of our outstanding students, Miss Adenike Adepetu, from the SS2 class, has achieved remarkable success in the highly esteemed “I Beg to Differ” debate competition.

Adenike Adepetu recently emerged as the first runner-up in the regional category of the 7th edition of the prestigious competition. Her exceptional eloquence, critical thinking skills, and dedication have rightfully earned her this prestigious position. We extend our heartfelt congratulations to Adenike for this remarkable achievement, which reflects her hard work, perseverance, and intellectual prowess.

Organized yearly by Nigeria Info 99.3 FM, the “I Beg to Differ” debate competition is renowned for its rigorous standards and competitive nature. Adenike’s success is a testament to her exceptional talent and commitment to excellence. We commend her for representing UPSS Comprehensive College with such distinction and grace.

Moreover, we are delighted to announce that she has also secured her place in the nationals, which will be held later this year. This remarkable accomplishment underscores her exceptional abilities and promises an exciting journey ahead. We have no doubt that she will continue to shine brightly on the national stage, showcasing her exceptional debating skills and representing our school with pride.

In recognition of her outstanding performance, she has been awarded a prize of ₦500 thousand and some electronic gadgets. This well-deserved reward is a testament to her dedication and talent, and we are immensely proud to see her achievements being recognized in such a meaningful way.

As a school community, we take great pride in celebrating the achievements of our students. This success serves as an inspiration to all of us, reminding us of the importance of perseverance, dedication, and a commitment to excellence.

We extend our sincere gratitude to Adenike for her hard work and dedication, as well as to her teachers and mentors who have supported and guided her throughout her journey. We also thank the parents and supporters who have encouraged and cheered her on every step of the way.

Please join us in congratulating Adenike Adepetu on her remarkable achievement and wishing her the very best as she prepares for the nationals. Let us continue to support and celebrate the success of all our students as they strive for excellence in their academic and extracurricular pursuits.
