Welcome

UPSS Comprehensive College has a long tradition of excellence in serving the educational needs of young individuals in Nigeria. We believe that every child can succeed and we work very hard to ensure that they do.

We offer quality and all-around education which provides students with academic excellence and good character. Education without character is nothing, thats why we instill sound moral and social values in our students.

We prepare our students to pursue their future dreams and goals by providing a solid academic foundation and a holistic education where students of all academic abilities are helped to achieve their potential. This is coupled with a broad extra-curricular programme that reaches out to the many varied interests of all students.

With an excellent team of dedicated, intelligent, and hardworking staff, our students are well-equipped with skills and confidence to face the future.

We look forward to welcoming you to UPSS Comprehensive College.

Academic Curricular

Our curriculum is based on the Nigerian educational system, having three junior sections (JSS1 JSS3) and three senior sections (SSS1 SSS3).

We offer the following subjects:

  1. Languages (English, French, Yoruba)
  2. Arts (History, Government, Literature, Christian Religious Studies)
  3. Science and Mathematics (Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Mathematics, Further Mathematics, Computer Science, Technical Drawing, Basic Science and Technology)
  4. Social Sciences (Economics, Geography, National Value Education)
  5. Vocational Studies (Agricultural Science, Home Economics)
  6. Business Studies (Financial Accounting, Commerce, Book Keeping)

Co-Curricular

We offer the following Curriculum Enrichment subjects such as:

  • Financial literacy where students are taught personal finance and money management skills.
  • Study skills (learning how to learn) where students are taught how to study.
  • Critical thinking where students are taught to think clearly, logically, and independently to proffer solutions to problems.
  • Leadership skills
  • Character Education

Extracurricular activities: Clubs and societies (Jet club, Chess club, press club, swimming, etc.
