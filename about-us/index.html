About Us

Our goal is to continue to produce well rounded students who will be able to compete anywhere in the world. We shall continue to teach good character by modelling and fostering compassion, respect, responsibility and integrity. In addition, our students will be challenged to pursue knowledge, creativity, and intellectual self reliance. We engage in community development and understand students right and the responsibilities within a community that is itself part of the larger world.

Our Mission

Our mission at UPSS Academy is to teach, inspire, transform and nurture our students with knowledge and skills so that they grow to become responsible young adults who can meet the challenges of the 21stcentury with confidence, persistence and resilience that will make them stand out wherever they may find themselves.

Our Vision

To create one of the states most effective schools helping secondary students prepare for and be accepted into top post secondary institutions.

School Philosophy

  • We believe firmly that all our students can become successful learners. Hence, we are committed to helping them discover and pursue their interests and in the process, discover who they are and what kind of excellence they possesses.
  • We believe that children learn best when they are actively engaged in their day to day learning and where they are given considerable responsibility for their studies.
  • We believe that when children enjoy school, they will make accelerated progress towards intellectual maturity.
  • We value what children achieve rather than focus on their failures.
  • We also believe in the need to nurture and develop our staff both professionally and personally. Since they are the one that create the climate in which our children will learn.
